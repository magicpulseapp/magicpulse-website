FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Which parks are supported?
Walt Disney World, Disneyland Resort, Tokyo Disney Resort, and Disneyland Paris. Wait times, hours, and park info for all four resorts in one app.
Is Magic Pulse free?
Yes. The app is free to download and use. You get live wait times, park hours, weather, and basic features. Pro adds alerts, widgets, Live Activity, Siri, wait history, no ads, and more.
What’s the difference between free and Pro?
Free includes live wait times, park hours, weather, and core planning. Pro adds watchlist summaries, push alerts (low wait, ride closure, park opening, etc.), home screen widgets, Live Activity on Lock Screen, Siri & Shortcuts, wait history and predictions, high refresh rate, no ads, and accent color. You can choose a one-time lifetime purchase or a weekly subscription; lifetime includes all future Pro features.
Where does wait time data come from?
Wait times and park hours are sourced from ThemeParks Wiki. Weather comes from OpenWeatherMap when configured. We don’t operate the parks; we surface live data so you can plan better.
Is Magic Pulse affiliated with Disney?
No. Magic Pulse is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by The Walt Disney Company. It’s an independent app that uses publicly available park data.
