Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Magic Pulse ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data we use

  • Location: Magic Pulse may use your device's location to suggest a nearby park and to send optional alerts (e.g. when you arrive at a resort or park, or for ride closures). Location is used on your device and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties for advertising or identification.
  • Notifications: If you enable notifications in Settings, we use them only to send the alerts you opted into (e.g. low wait times, park opening, ride closures). You can turn these off at any time in Settings.
  • On-device data: Preferences, watchlist, and usage history used for features like wait predictions are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to our servers.

Data we do not sell

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your location or identity for third-party advertising.

Third-party services

  • Weather: Weather data is provided by Apple Weather (WeatherKit). Data sources and attribution. Apple’s use of data is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Wait times and park data: Sourced from ThemeParks Wiki. No personal data is sent beyond what is needed to request park data (e.g. park identifier).
  • Analytics or ads: If the app uses analytics or advertising, those providers may collect data according to their policies; we do not control that collection.

In-app purchases

Pro upgrades (lifetime or weekly subscription) are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment details. Subscription management is handled in your device Settings and your App Store or Google Play account.

Children

Magic Pulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via the support link on our App Store or Google Play listing, or at the email address listed there.
